Zen Crystals

We invite you to sit back and allow yourself to relax as you listen to the music and play.

The object of this game is to match your score to the randomly generated number. As you click one of the 4 crystals in the middile of the display you will see you score begin to climb. Each of the crystals has a random value of which you must be mindful.

Be careful not to let your score pass the random number or you will lose the round.

After 10 losses the game is over and you must start your journey again.


Once you are ready to proceed, click OK.
